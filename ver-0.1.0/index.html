ABOUT

About Us

Godet & Young is one of the oldest hardware stores in Bermuda. Located in St. George's, we are the only hardware store on the east end of the island. We provide numerous services and stock a plethora of products, ranging from screws and bolts to lawn mowers, coolers, and paint. Our staff are friendly, experienced, and accommodate all customers in order to meet their needs. Have hardware questions or inquiries? Call our shop at (441) 297-1940 or visit us at 27 Wellington Street in St. Georges.

Captain Smokes' Marina

Connected (not physically) to Godet & Young Hardware is Captain Smokes' Marina. Located 2 minutes away from the store, Captain Smokes Marina offers the most competitive pricing and well maintained facilities located on a private dock in St. Georges. Captain Smokes has been providing med mooring docking since 1986 and are proud of a record of annual repeat business. At Captain Smokes you will find friendly assistance and advice to meet your needs while in Bermuda.

Any questions regarding the marina can be answered at our store location: 27 Wellington Street or by calling the shop at (441) 297-1940.
