About

Our History

Established since 1880, Godet & Young has provided helpful and friendly hardware services to the island community. Originally established in the city of Hamilton on front street, a branch was soon established in the town of St. George’s. After the closing of the Hamilton branch and its main competitor - St. George’s Hardware - Godet & Young became the sole hardware provider in the east end.

Later purchased by Major Moore and Edwin Hayward in the 1970s Godet & Young soon became a limited company with a number of shareholders. After becoming the majority shareholder, Robert Oatley became the current managing director and has continued the goal of providing useful hardware and friendly customer service to the east end community.




The Storefront

Often enough our store provides service to customers from all areas of the island thanks to the wide amount and diversity of products we carry. Our store provides a wide assortment of hardware, plumbing supplies, carpentry supplies, painting supplies, electrical supplies, and tools for any job.



Our Staff

Godet & Young is home to the most experienced hardware juggernauts on the island. Still on staff after working for the shop for at least 20 years each: Robert Oatley, Gerry Ryan, and Edwin Hayward continue to offer their timeless advice to the community.
